India is a country located in South Asia and is the seventh-largest country by area in the world.
With a population of over 1.3 billion people, India is the second-most populous country in the world.
India has a rich cultural and historical heritage, with diverse traditions, languages, and religions.
The economy of India is one of the fastest-growing in the world, with a large service sector and a growing
manufacturing industry.
India is also known for its contributions to fields such as mathematics, astronomy, literature, and art,
and has produced notable figures such as Mahatma Gandhi, Rabindranath Tagore, and Satyajit Ray.
